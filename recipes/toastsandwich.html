Toast Sandwich

Description

Pinnacle of haute cuisine, the French will try to say they didn't invent it but they 100% did,

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • Another slice of bread
  • A final slice of bread
  • Mystery amount of butter if you're feeling fancy

Steps

  1. Look in your cupboard and realise you only have half a loaf of bread in and no other food
  2. Apply butter to two of the slices of bread.
  3. Place the remaining slice in a toaster, walk around, forget its in the toaster, and get back just in time to pop it up before it's cremated
  4. Put the toasted bread in between the untoasted slices, and garnish to serve.
